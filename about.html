About Auditory Therapy

Supporting hearing rehabilitation through accessible technology

Our Story

This website was born from a personal journey with hearing rehabilitation. When someone close to us received cochlear implants, we witnessed firsthand the challenges of auditory therapy sessions.

The parents who were helping with therapy sessions found themselves mentally exhausted from constantly having to think of random words and sentences. They needed to provide varied, unpredictable content while also focusing on assessing the user's hearing performance.

We realized that this challenge wasn't unique to one family. Many people going through auditory therapy face the same difficulties, and their support systems often struggle with the mental load of creating effective practice sessions.

Our Mission

We created this tool to:

  • Reduce mental fatigue for therapy assistants by automating word and sentence selection
  • Improve session quality through structured, randomized content delivery
  • Track progress with detailed statistics and session history
  • Make therapy accessible to anyone with a web browser, completely free
  • Support consistency in practice sessions for better outcomes

Who Can Benefit

People with Cochlear Implants

Structured practice sessions to improve sound recognition and speech comprehension after implantation.

Hearing Aid Users

Regular auditory training to maximize the benefits of hearing aid technology and improve listening skills.

Family Members & Caregivers

Easy-to-use tools that remove the burden of creating therapy content while maintaining session effectiveness.

Speech Therapists

Professional-grade session tracking and progress monitoring for clinical or home-based therapy programs.
